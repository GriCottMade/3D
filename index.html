GriCott

I am offering 3D printing and design/prototyping services. This page is a quick setup for friends and family to see what I'm offering and to inspire how you can use these services.

  • 3D Printing Services

3D Printing Services

You provide a 3d printable file that either you designed or downloaded from one of the sites below that offer thousands of free designs.

  • Thingiverse (free)
  • Printables (free)
  • Thangs (free and paid)
  • Cults (free and paid)

I will print the file in the color of your choosing and a material suitable to your needs. My price for this service is $3.00 per hour of printing time with a 5 hour minimum per order with unlimited items per order, plus shipping. If design requires any print in-place harware (bearings, screws, magnets) you will be charged cost of the hardware.

Friends and family will get a 50% discount along with zero minium if we can meet up to deliver the items. Email gricottmade@gmail.com the file(s), or link to file(s) to get started.

  • Material and Color Options

Materials and Colors

Skies the limit when it comes to colors and materials and for the most part I can offer to print in all of them except some of the polycarbonates and nylons. Here is a good article on material types

Beyond the basic plastics there are flexible, wood-filled (can be sanded and stained), metal-filled (can be rusted, oxidized ect...), conductive, glow-in-the-dark, magnetic, carbon fiber, color-changing and dichromatic varients. If you have a specific need just contact me and we will find the right material for you.

  • Design and Prototyping

Design and Protoyping Services

Have something old missing a piece that could be printed, have an idea for some new product or simply a very unique design in your head you want to turn into reality. We can work together to make it happen at a very affordable price. Just email gricottmade@gmail.com to get started.
